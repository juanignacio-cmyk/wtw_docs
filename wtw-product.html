WtW — Accessibility Verified Product

What It Is

Annual program for destinations and hospitality businesses to achieve the trusted standard for accessibility. Combines expert fieldwork + standardized methodology + digital tools.

The Problem They Solve

  • $120B global accessible travel market, $25B in US alone
  • 13M US travelers with disabilities, 38M trips/year
  • 74% encounter accessibility obstacles — mainly due to MISSING or UNRELIABLE INFORMATION
  • $19B wasted annually due to information gap
  • ADA mandates infrastructure, NOT information
  • Generic "accessible" labels are useless — needs vary widely (wheelchair vs blind vs elderly)

The Product = Accessibility Data Layer

  • 200+ standardized data points per site
  • Covers: hotels, attractions, restaurants, cruise lines, airports, convention centers
  • On-ground verification by trained "Mappers" using Accessibility Mapping System App

How It Works

  • Verify: Mappers visit destination, assess 200+ data points per location
  • Data → Bookable Inventory: Published on WtW ecosystem + distributed via hotel websites, OTAs, metasearch, AI tools
  • Market: Destination pages, curated guides, campaigns targeting disability + senior communities

3 Value Pillars for DMOs

  • Market: Increase visitation from disability + senior travelers via SEO landing pages, WtW community, destination hubs, AI trip planning
  • Verify: Measure and benchmark accessibility with standardized assessments, dashboards, partner portal, annual improvement roadmaps
  • Improve: Ongoing support, workshops, WTW Academy, enablement toolkits, performance tracking

Revenue Model

  • Annual recurring program (DV = Destination Verified)
  • Media Solutions (MS) = marketing campaigns (separate product, project-based)
  • Sweet spot: $10-50K annual for DV

Key Selling Points

  • "Gold standard for accessibility information"
  • Capture $120B market
  • Reduce lawsuits/refunds from misinformation
  • Brand equity / DEI / sustainability alignment
  • Data distributed across multiple channels (not just WtW)
